# Throughline Copy Brief for Yuzheng Sun's Personal Homepage

## What This Brief Is For

This is a copywriting and positioning challenge for the personal homepage of Yuzheng Sun.

The immediate problem is to rewrite the "throughline" headline on the homepage. The deeper problem is to express the narrative spine of the whole site in one complete, natural sentence.

The desired output is not a slogan, not a list of themes, and not a three-part framework. It should read like a complete thought from a real person.

## Core Challenge

Current concepts keep collapsing into three stacked elements:

- Better models of reality.
- Faster experiments.
- Work / systems that compound.

The user explicitly does not want this to feel like three ingredients placed next to each other. The sentence needs a subject, movement, and consequence. It should express the whole arc in one complete idea.

Bad pattern:

> Better models of reality. Faster experiments. Work that compounds.

Also bad, because it is still mostly a chain of components:

> From economics to experimentation to AI-native building.

Also still not right:

> Better models of reality, through faster experiments, become work or systems that compound.

Why this fails:

It names the right components, but it still feels like a mechanical transformation formula. It does not yet sound like a personal intellectual throughline, lived career arc, or distinctive worldview.

## Current Page Context

The homepage is not intended to be a conventional landing page. It should help readers quickly understand Yuzheng as a person, thinker, builder, and teacher.

The page should feel more like a high-resolution self-introduction:

- What kind of person is this?
- What question has he been working on across different jobs and fields?
- What does he believe?
- Why do his courses, community, books, interviews, company sessions, and guest network belong to the same story?

The page should not feel like:

- a product marketing page
- a generic AI educator homepage
- a trophy wall
- a resume pasted into sections
- a pile of impressive but disconnected proof points

## Yuzheng's Actual Arc

The throughline needs to make sense of this path:

- Cornell: PhD in Economics
- Amazon: Economist
- Meta / Facebook: Data Scientist, growth, experimentation, A/B testing
- Tencent IEG: Director / Deputy Director, Data & AI, managing a data and AI team
- Statsig: Principal Data Scientist and sole evangelist, early member before OpenAI acquisition
- Superlinear Academy / AI-Builders: Founder & CEO, building courses, community, company training, interviews, and learning systems for AI-era builders

The important connective tissue is not "I moved from economics to data to AI."

The stronger idea is that each stage sharpened a way of working:

- economics trains models of incentives, constraints, and causality
- experimentation turns opinions into facts
- growth and A/B testing make learning operational
- management forces judgment to survive inside real organizations
- AI-native building makes feedback loops cheaper and faster
- teaching and community turn those patterns into systems other people can use

It still feels like "better models + faster experiments + compounding systems" as three elements glued together. The user asked for one complete sentence with a beginning and end, not a disguised list.

English:

- plain but strong
- no startup fluff
- no self-mythologizing
- no empty grandiosity
- no generic "empowering people in the AI era"
- should feel like good personal homepage writing, not landing-page copy

## Important User Preferences

The user wants the homepage to be narrative, not marketing.

He wants it to feel like:

> 娓娓道来的自我介绍：这是我做过的一些事，这是我的成长轨迹，这是我学到的东西，这是我的主张。

He does not want the "English network" or famous guests to be shown in an obvious, status-seeking way. The same applies to the throughline: it should reveal depth naturally, not insist on it.

He also wants growth and A/B testing to be present because they are a real professional passion, not just resume details.

## What the Sentence Needs to Do

The throughline headline should ideally:

- explain why economics, experimentation, growth, AI, teaching, and community belong together
- feel personal rather than abstract
- imply an intellectual obsession or recurring question
- show movement from understanding reality to acting on it
- include the idea of compounding only if it feels earned
- avoid sounding like a framework with three pillars
- be strong enough to sit as the headline of the "throughline" section

## Potential Directions to Explore

These are not final answers. They are directions for brainstorming.

### Direction 1: A recurring question

Frame the throughline as a question he has carried across fields.

Example shape:

> I have kept asking how people can see reality more clearly, test their judgment sooner, and turn the answer into something that keeps working after the first insight.

Problem:

This still risks becoming a three-part list unless written carefully.

### Direction 2: Reality as the center

Make reality the anchor, not "models / experiments / systems."

Example shape:

> My work has always been about shortening the distance between what people believe, what reality proves, and what they are able to build from it.

Why it may work:

It has one conceptual movement: shortening the distance.

Risk:

May be too abstract.

### Direction 3: Judgment as the center

The core theme might be better judgment, not models.

Example shape:

> I have spent my career learning how judgment gets formed, tested, and turned into real-world leverage.

Why it may work:

It reads like a complete sentence and connects economics, experimentation, product, management, AI, and education.

Risk:

"Real-world leverage" may sound a little generic.

### Direction 4: Learning loops as the center

The real theme may be feedback loops.

Example shape:

> Across economics, growth, and AI, I keep building ways for people to learn from reality faster than their assumptions can harden.

Why it may work:

It has a sharper opinion and avoids a simple three-part enumeration.

Risk:

May understate the "building systems / compounding work" part.

### Direction 5: Superlinear Growth as the implicit theme

Do not necessarily use "Superlinear Growth" as the headline. Treat it as the underlying idea:

> Growth becomes superlinear when better judgment, faster feedback, and useful systems start reinforcing one another.

Why it may work:

It explains the brand idea.

Risk:

It may sound too slogan-like and less personal unless anchored in "my work."

## Stronger Candidate Shapes

These are closer to the desired form because each has one grammatical and conceptual movement.

1. My work has always been about shortening the distance between how people understand reality and what they can build from it.

2. I have spent my career looking for the loops that turn clearer judgment into faster learning and better work.

3. The common thread is learning how good judgment survives contact with reality, then becomes something other people can use.

4. Across economics, experimentation, growth, and AI, I keep returning to the same problem: how to make learning from reality faster, sharper, and more useful.

5. I build systems that help people update their models of reality quickly enough to turn judgment into compounding work.

6. The path only looks varied from the outside; underneath, I have been studying how people turn clearer models of the world into decisions, products, and systems that keep improving.

These are also not final. They are meant to help another model understand the target.

4. 我做过的事情看起来分散，底层其实都在回答一个问题：怎样把对现实更好的理解，变成更好的决策、产品和系统。

5. 我真正感兴趣的，是判断如何在现实中被验证，又如何变成别人也能使用的能力。

6. 如果说有一条主线，那就是我一直在研究怎样让好的判断离现实更近，也离行动更近。

## Possible Best Current Direction

The most promising English direction may be:

> My work has always been about shortening the distance between how people understand reality and what they can build from it.

Why:

- It is one complete sentence.
- It avoids listing "models, experiments, systems."
- It contains the deeper idea behind economics, experimentation, growth, and AI.
- It feels personal without being self-important.

Potential Chinese companion:

> 我一直关心的是：人怎样更清楚地理解现实，并把这种理解变成真正能被使用、能持续变好的东西。

However, this may still be too broad. A stronger final version should preserve this sentence's unity while making it more specific to experimentation, growth, and AI-native building.
